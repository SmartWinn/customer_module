3. What to do in case of disaster?
✓ Report the accident to Carabineros
✓ Give notice to the insurer within 10 days.
How to make the complaint in Carabineros?
Describe how the events occurred, delivering the data of the affected third party (name of the driver, RUT, patent, telephone and insurer, if any)
- Where to do it? In the police station closest to the incident and corresponding to the same commune where the accident occurred.
- How to do it? You must present your identity card and the documentation of the car (standard, patent, etc.).
Notification to the insurer
Then, you must give notice in the insurer within 10 days of the incident. For that you need the following documents:
✓ Transitory license or permission
✓ Constancy in Carabineros
✓ Police number
✓ Number of the Court that processes the case
